Hockey Career!

  • 1965: Steve is born on May 9th
  • 1986-1987: Became Captain of the Detroit Redwings
  • 1988-1989: Steve recorded 155 points for the season
  • 1997: Stanley Cup Victory
  • 1998: Stanley Cup Victory
  • 2002: Stanley Cup Victory
  • 2003: Knee surgery has him missing a large chunk of regular season
  • 2004: Eye Surgery to repair broken orbital bone and scratched cornea
  • 2005: Steve signs a one year contract with Detroit
  • 2006: Steve announces his retirement form professional Hockey

Steve Yzerman's humilty in the midst of all of his success is really what stands out.

He has always shown himself to be a fair player, gracious with his teammates, yet determined to be a champion.
